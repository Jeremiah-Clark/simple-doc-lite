# Writing Content

Standard Markdown works everywhere: **bold**, *italic*, ~strikethrough~,
`inline code`, [links](https://example.com), lists, and tables.

## Callouts

Use GitHub-style alerts to highlight information:

> [!NOTE]
> This is a note. Write the marker on the first line and your text on
> the following lines, exactly like on GitHub.

> [!TIP] You can add a custom title
> The title goes on the marker line; the body starts on the next line.

Available types: `NOTE`, `TIP`, `WARNING`, `IMPORTANT`, `CAUTION`,
`SUMMARY`, `EXAMPLE`.
